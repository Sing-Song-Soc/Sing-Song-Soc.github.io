Summer 2013

The Glee Summer 2013 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7pm, on Tuesday 25th June.

The theme was, theoretically, based on regions - America, Europe, Middle Earth, Africa and Israel.
In the end, not every song fit into a theme, and some of the ones that ostensibly did were pretty tenuous (James - "Rosanna is by Toto, whose most famous song is 'Africa'")

Musical Directors: James Taylor and Craig Perkinton
