'A Night at the Movies!' - Summer 2015

The Glee Summer 2015 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 18th June.
The concert was titled 'A Night at the Movies!', and I imagine you can probably guess the theme.
Astonishingly, nearly every song fitted the theme, a Glee record.
Songs from this concert are on YouTube - playlist here.

Musical Director: Peter Brohan, Craig Perkinton, Katie Privett
Accompanist: James Taylor
