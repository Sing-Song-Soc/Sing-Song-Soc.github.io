'What's in a Name?' - Summer 2018

The Glee Summer 2018 concert took place at St Deny's Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 14th June.
The concert was entitled 'What's in a Name?', with songs containing names, mostly.

Musical Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
