'Count Me In And Spell It Out' - Winter 2022

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2022 concert took place in RCH/037 (Ron Cooke Hub), at 4pm, on Sunday 27th November.
It was titled 'Count Me In And Spell It Out', with the theme of counting and spelling. It was also joked that there was an unintentional third theme of stars.

Music Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
Assistant Music Director: Izzie Wraith-Haycock
