Christmas 2006

The Glee Christmas 2006 concert took place at St. Oswald's Church, at 7.30pm, on Saturday 9th December.

Musical Director: J. Mark Pim
Assistant Musical Director: Fiona Constantine

Choir

Soprano

Alto

Tenor

Bass

Programme

The Lord Is My Shepherd (Carter)

A British Tar (Sullivan arr. Gritton)

Christ Has No Body Now But Yours (Ogden)

Let Thy Merciful Ears (Mudd)

Just Another Star (arr. Gritton)

Interval

O Holy Night (Adam arr. West)
- Soloists : Lauren and Stuart

Lute-book Lullaby (Ballet arr. Shaw)

Coelos Ascendit Hodie (Stanford)

Bridge Over Troubled Water (Simon)

Mary's Boy Child (arr. Gritton)
