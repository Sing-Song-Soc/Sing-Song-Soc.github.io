Cosmic Christmas - Summer 2025

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2025 concert took place in ATB/056, at 4pm, on Sunday 7th December.
It was titled 'Cosmic Christmas', with a mix of space-themed and christmas songs.

Music Directors: Lily Penny
Assistant Music Directors: Izzie Wraith-Haycock, Chris Jacobs, EJ Nyren, Anna Harvey
Accompanists: Lily Penny, Chris Jacobs
