'Raising Voices' - Spring 2023

The Sing Song Soc Spring 2023 concert took place in RCH/037 (Ron Cooke Hub), at 4pm, on Sunday 12th March.
It was titled 'Raising Voices', with the theme of highlighting voices that aren't typically heard on the radio.

Music Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
Assistant Music Director: Izzie Wraith-Haycock
