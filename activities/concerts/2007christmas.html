Christmas 2007

The Glee Christmas 2007 concert took place at St. Oswald's Church, at 7.30pm, on Friday 7th December.
The concert was performed alongside the St. Oswald's Church Choir, who performed a selection of carols to open the concert.

Musical Director: Matthew Ferguson
Assistant Musical Director: J. Mark Pim
