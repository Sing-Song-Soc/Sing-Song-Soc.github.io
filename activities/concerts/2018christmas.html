'A Spaceman Came Travelling' - Christmas 2018

The Glee Christmas 2018 concert took place at St Deny's Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 28th November.
The concert was entitled 'A Spaceman Came Travelling', with songs about space and Christmas and one song about both space and Christmas.

Musical Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
