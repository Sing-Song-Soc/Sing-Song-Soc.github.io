'By the Water' - Summer 2016

The Glee Summer 2016 concert took place at St Denys Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 9th June.
The concert was entitled 'By the Water', with the songs (almost) all accomodating an aquatic aspect.
In keeping with the theme, the concert raised money in support of Water Aid.
This concert is on YouTube - playlist here.
