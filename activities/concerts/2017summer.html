'True Colours' - Summer 2017

The Glee Summer 2017 concert took place at St Denys Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 15th June.
The concert was entitled 'True Colours', a colection of songs on the theme of colours.
This concert is on YouTube - playlist here.

This was Katie's last concert as MD, and the choir celebrated her tenure with a surprise encore of a favourite piece, 'The Long Day Closes', which is only coincidentally about death. And also not about colours, but we let that pass in the circumstances.1

Musical Director: Katie Privett
