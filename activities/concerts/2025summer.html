35th Anniversary - Summer 2025

The Sing Song Soc Summer 2025 concert took place in the Roger Kirk Centre, at 4pm, on Sunday 8th June.
It was titled '35th Anniversary', focusing on the Choir's history and themed around past themes the choir has done.

Music Directors: Lily Penny
Assistant Music Directors: Izzie Wraith-Haycock, Chris Jacobs, EJ Nyren, Anna Harvey
Accompanists: Lily Penny, Chris Jacobs
