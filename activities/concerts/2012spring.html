Spring 2012

The Glee Spring 2012 concert took place at St. Helen's Church, at 7.30pm, on Tuesday 13th March.

A selection of songs from this concert can be heard here! (Incorrectly labelled as the Spring 2011 concert)

Weslie created a beautiful video on the subject of this concert.
There was also a link to a video about a social, but this no longer works :(
