'A Book by the Fire' - Christmas 2016

The Glee Christmas 2016 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 1st December.
The concert was entitled 'A Book by the Fire', with the songs theoretically united by literature, fire and Christmas.

Musical Director: Katie Privett
Accompanist: Joe Griffiths
