'Throwback Disco' - Summer 2024

The Sing Song Soc Summer 2024 concert took place in James Hall, at 4pm, on Sunday 9th June.
It was titled 'Throwback Disco', with the theme being any song that is nostalgic to us, with a Year 6 disco vibe to the whole thing.

Music Directors: Izzie Wraith-Haycock, Lily Penny
Assistant Music Director: Chris Jacobs, Abi Harrison
Accompanist: Ani Hurford, Chris Jacobs
