'All Is Fair In Love And War' - Spring 2015

The Glee Spring 2015 concert took place at St Helen's Church, at 7pm, on Thursday 5th March.
The concert was titled 'All Is Fair In Love And War', with the first half representing love, the second war.

Musical Directors: Peter Brohan, Craig Perkinton, Katie Privett
Accompanist: Imogen Breen
