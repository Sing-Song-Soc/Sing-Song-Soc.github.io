'Christmas Spirit' - Christmas 2017

The Glee Christmas 2017 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 30th November.
The concert was entitled 'Christmas Spirit', with the songs about drinking, Christmas and drinking at Christmas.

Musical Director: Ani Hurford
