Spring 2006

The Glee Spring 2006 concert took place at St. Oswald's Church, at 7.30pm, on Monday 13th March.

We came very close to singing Lay A Garland, but this was one of the many terms in which it defeated us. I don't recall if we ever actually finished it, but we started it quite a few times.

Not much info on this term, I'm sure it exists somewhere. - This seems increasingly unlikely but we can hope!
