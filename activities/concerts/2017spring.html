'Night and Day' - Spring 2017

The Glee Spring 2017 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 16th March.
The concert was entitled 'Night and Day', with the theme of… night and day.
Songs from this concert are on YouTube - playlist here.

Musical Director: Katie Privett
Accompanist: Craig Perkinton
