'A Much Needed Break' - Spring 2022

The Sing Song Soc Spring 2022 concert took place in RCH/037 (Ron Cooke Hub), at 7.30pm, on Friday 11th March.
It was titled 'A Much Needed Break'.

Musical Director: Josh Cameron

Programme

Unfortunately we do not currently have a full programme for this concert, however we do have a list of group numbers:

Man! I Feel Like a Woman! - Shania Twain (arranged and conducted by Josh Cameron)

My Church - Maren Morris (arranged and conducted by Josh Cameron)

The Road Home - Michael Dennis Browne (adapted by Stephen Paulus, conducted by Josh Cameron)

Name of the Game - ABBA (arranged and conducted by Ani Hurford)

Good Vibrations - The Beach Boys (arranged and conducted by Josh Cameron)

Finale - Rebecca Sugar, Gallant Avi and Surasshu (arranged and conducted by Brian Courtehoute)
