'Lights, Camera, Chaos!' - Spring 2024

The Sing Song Soc Spring 2024 concert took place in BS/005 (Berrick Saul), at 4pm, on Sunday 24th March.
It was titled 'Lights, Camera, Chaos!', with a theme of songs from musicals, TV, and movies. Our theme for outfits was Barbies and Kens.

Music Director: Izzie Wraith-Haycock
Assistant Music Director: Lily Penny, Chris Jacobs
Accompanist: Ani Hurford
