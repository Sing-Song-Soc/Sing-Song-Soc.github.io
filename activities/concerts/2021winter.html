'The Good Times, Bad Times Concert' - Winter 2021

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2021 concert took place in SLB/118 (Spring Lane Building), at 7.30pm, on Sunday 28th November.
It was titled 'The Good Times, Bad Times Concert'. This was the first concert after Stuart's passing and a tribute to him was on the back of the programme.
This was also the first concert back from COVID, having been over 20 months since the last concert.

Musical Director: Josh Cameron
Accompanist: Ani Hurford
