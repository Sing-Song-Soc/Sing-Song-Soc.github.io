'Winter Concert' - Winter 2021

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2021 concert took place in SLB/118, at 7.30pm, on Sunday 28th November.
It was titled 'Winter Concert', starting a trend of Winter Concerts, rather than Christmas ones. Also, from this point no concerts have been held in churches.
This was the first concert back from COVID, having been over 20 months since the last concert.

Musical Director: Josh Cameron
