'A Time and a Place' - Spring 2018

The Glee Spring 2018 concert took place at St Deny's Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 15th March.
The concert was entitled 'A Time and a Place', with the songs about, in a pretty broad interpretaion, times and places.

Musical Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
