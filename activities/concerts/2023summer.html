'YUrovision' - Summer 2023

The Sing Song Soc Summer 2023 concert took place in RCH/037 (Ron Cooke Hub), at 4pm, on Sunday 25th June.
It was titled 'YUrovision', a portmanteau of YU (York Uni) and Eurovision, with the majority of the songs being past Eurovision songs.

Music Director: Izzie Wraith-Haycock
Assistant Music Director: Genevieve Andrews
Accompanist: Ani Hurford
