'The Yuletide Concert' - Christmas 2019

The Sing Song Soc Christmas 2019 concert took place at Heslington Church, at 7.30pm, on Thursday 5th December.
The concert was entitled 'The Yuletide Concert', the theme sort of Christmassy - the carols were inserted ad hoc as the concert went on.

Musical Director and Accompanist: Ani Hurford
Assistant Musical Directors: Sebrina Stickney Morris, Josh Cameron
