'Cosy Under The Covers' - Winter 2023

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2023 concert took place in BS/005 (Berrick Saul), at 4pm, on Sunday 10th December.
It was titled 'Cosy Under The Covers', being comprised of mainly cover versions and wintery songs.

Music Director: Izzie Wraith-Haycock
Assistant Music Directors: Lily Penny & Chris Jacobs
Accompanist: Ani Hurford
