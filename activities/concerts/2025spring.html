Spring Showcase - Spring 2025

The Sing Song Soc Spring 2025 concert took place in BS/005 (Berrick Saul), at 4pm, on Sunday 2nd March.
It was titled 'Spring Showcase', being unthemed and focusing mainly on solos with a smaller number of group songs due to semesterisation.

Music Directors: Izzie Wraith-Haycock, Lily Penny
Assistant Music Directors: Abi Harrison, Chris Jacobs
