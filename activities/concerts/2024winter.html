'Mappy Christmas' - Winter 2024

The Sing Song Soc Winter 2024 concert took place in BS/005 (Berrick Saul), at 4pm, on Sunday 8th December.
It was titled 'Mappy Christmas', with most songs containing places and/or being Christmassy.

Music Directors: Lily Penny, Izzie Wraith-Haycock
Assistant Music Directors: Chris Jacobs, Abi Harrison
Accompanist: Lily Penny
