'Under My Umbrella' - Christmas 2015

The Glee Christmas 2015 concert took place at St Deny's Church, at 7.00pm, on Thursday 26th November.
The concert was entitled 'Under My Umbrella', with the theme of Christmas and weather.
This concert is on YouTube - playlist here.

Musical Director: Peter Brohan & Katie Privett
Accompanists: Margaret Griffiths & Rachel Emmerson
