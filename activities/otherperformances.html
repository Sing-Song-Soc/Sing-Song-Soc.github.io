Other Performances

Borders - 3rd December 2008

- On the evening of 3/12, Glee were invited to sing carols at Borders, at an event to mark the publishing of a new JK Rowling book. A good number of us gathered and sang and were rewarded with book tokens. After the concert, the choir were treated to a selection of books bought with the proceeds.
- We enjoyed working with the Borders people so much that we went back in a few times that term in smaller groups to sing and collect for charity.

Goodricke College Opening - 28th April 2010

- At the beginning of the term, we had a treat: Glee were invited to perform at the official opening of the new Goodricke college at Hes East.
- A small delegation sang 'Don't It Make My Brown Eyes Blue' for a selection of dignitaries (including HRH Prince Andrew) on the morning of Wednesday 28th April.
- The invitation was the result of the strong relationship between MD Anna Stephenson and the college administration.
- The lucky volunteers were Laurent Michaux, Stuart Roberts, Lucy Davies, Clare Rainsford, Katie Privett and Rachel Haynes, led by Anna Stephenson.
- A good time was had by all, and we liked the song so much we sang it again in the coming term's concert.
- An archived version of a York Vision article about the event can be found here, unfortunately there are no longer any pictures.
