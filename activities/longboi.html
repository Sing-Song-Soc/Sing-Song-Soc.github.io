Long Boi Memorial

On the 26th of September 2024, Greg James came to Central Hall to hold a funeral for a duck.
We sang a parody of Chappell Roan's HOT TO GO (L-O-N-G B-O-I) and See You Again by Wiz Khalifa, both live on BBC Radio 1.
It is one of the silliest things I've ever had the pleasure of being part of.

Music Directors: Lily Penny, Izzie Wraith-Haycock
