Residential 2006

Saturday 13th - Sunday 14th May.

We left York bright and early on Saturday, and headed to Cottonstones to learn and perform Pergelosi's Magnificat.
The church itself was undergoing repair, so we rehearsed and performed in the upstairs hall, or 'the pottle room', as we know it.
