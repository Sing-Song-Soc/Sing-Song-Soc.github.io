This list is a list of all known concert programmes and choirs.

Everything between Christmas 2005 and Spring 2020 is taken from the wikidot where it says:
Very much a work in progress, with quite a few concerts missing lists of singers, and one or two missing programmes. Doubtless some solos have been either misattributed or unattributed, if anyone spots any gaps, feel free to fix them. Obviously, nothing yet before Christmas 2005, but I'm sure we can fix that.

If you have any information about concerts that is not on this page PLEASE email either Sing Song (email at bottom of page) or me directly at freyacgoodger@gmail.com. We'd love to hear about it and add it to the archive :)
