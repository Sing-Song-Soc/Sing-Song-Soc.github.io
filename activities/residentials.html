Residentials

We try to do a residential every year, this is as complete a list of past residentials as I can put together.

Cottonstones Residentials

Many early residentials were held at Cottonstones, it currently seems that the last time we went was 2012.

The Glee weekend away!

We visit the church of St. Mary's at Cottonstones, not very far from Halifax, but for our purposes in the middle of nowhere. Aside from the lovely (and surprisingly warm) church, there is a wonderful pub and… nothing else, really.

A very important element of the residential is Pottle, the official Glee game, devised and played almost exclusively at Cottonstones.
