Maddie Jones

Sing Song Member: 2021-2025

Committee Roles
Secretary: 2022-2024

"I have always had a love of singing and performing, but I've never had a choir that was so special and meaningful to me as Sing Song Society. I initially joined as a fresher who wanted to sing some Christmas carols, and I stayed for the incredible people and friends I had made - and to keep the society running as its Secretary!

There are so many brilliant memories associated with Sing Song Soc - marching onstage in full Village People gear to do the YMCA, trying to keep a straight face during It's Raining Men while Brian goes ham on the kazoo behind me, and going to the club decked out in the most horrendous pieces from charity shops.

But whether I'm making an absolute fool of myself by learning a BTS dance for Dynamite, or solemnly parodying Chappell Roan in memorial to a duck, I always give my all. Because at the end of the day, this society brings so much joy to myself and others, so you have to commit to the silliness!"
