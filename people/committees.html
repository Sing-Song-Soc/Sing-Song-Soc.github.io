Committees

A list of who was running things, and when.

Committee elections take place at the AGM in Spring, with a new committee coming into power after the Easter break and remaining for a year.

Glee elections have formerly took place at the end of each year, at around Christmas concert time, helpfully leaving each committee in charge over the course of a calender year. From 2016, they will move to the Spring, the committee in place serving a slightly extended term.1

List of committee roles, new and old, can be seen here.
