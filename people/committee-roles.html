Committee Roles

This page contains all sorts of information about different committee roles the society has had.

Current Committee Roles (2024/25)

Below is a list of all current committee roles and a brief description of what they do.

Signatories

President - Leader of the society, generally ensures everyone is doing what they need to be doing. Chairs meetings, gives speeches, usually in charge of the residential. The friendly face of Sing Song Society.

Secretary - Sends out the weekly email with information about rehearsals, socials, concerts, and anything else relevant. In charge of room bookings for rehearsals and concerts. Generally in charge of admin.

Treasurer - In charge of the society's budget and money. Applies for grants, tracks expenditure, gets refreshments for concerts, pays invoices, sets up fundraising pages.

Music Director - Runs rehearsals, decides set lists/themes, sources or arranges sheet music, organises soloists and accompaniment, makes music available to all members through Google Drive.

Other Committee Members

Assistant Music Director - Aids the Music Director with the musical side of the society where possible, contributes to discussions of concert themes.

Press and Publicity - Manage society Instagram account, assist in running YouTube page, design programmes for concerts and print them, help notify society of events, make sure socials are promoted on social media.

Librarian - Look after all the physical music, print sheet music, assist with the Google Drive, make sure equipment is in place for rehearsals and concerts, ensure every member has all the music for the current term. You usually want two librarians.

Social Secretary - Determine what sort of socials the choir wants, organise and plan socials, make sure signatories and Press&Pub are aware of socials, book rooms for socials/liaise with the secretary to do so, host or co-host socials and ensure rooms are kept tidy/people go to the right places.

Accessibility Officer - Make sure everything is accessible as possible, be available as an approachable check-in point for all society members, liaise with social sec and press and pub to deliver accessibility guide for all socials.

Legacy Officer - Look after the Sing Song Society archive and the Society's filming equipment, organise the filming and streaming of concerts, edit concert footage for storage and store videos on the society Youtube page and the society drive.

Legacy Roles

Below is a list of previous roles that are not currently in use.

Chair - Chairs meetings, obviously. Almost everything else can be delegated to someone else if you want. Traditionally, has contacted and negotiated with concert venues. Usually the main point of contact with St Mary's in Cottonstones, and is theoretically in charge over all of the weekend away. Keeps an eye on the committee and makes sure everything that needs to be done is being done by someone. Above all else, the friendly face of Glee, and our leader, although the amount of speeches necessary is entirely negotiable.

Webmaster - Deals mainly with websites.

Merchandise - Deals mainly with merchandise.

Ordinary Member - Deals with anything else. Booking rooms, moving things about, all sorts of things, especially if any of the other positions are currently unfilled.

Feedback Officer - In charge of polls and unspecific society queries and checks our rehearsals, concerts and socials work well in an Equality, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI) lens. Preferably held by a new member so we have a committee voice directly telling us what might be confusing for a new member.

Legacy Descriptions

Below is a list of role descriptions for current roles that were taken from the wikidot.

Secretary - Deals mainly with communication. In charge of keeping an up to date membership list and maintaining the e-mail list. Generally deals with the e-mail account - responding to queries etc. Sends all messages out to the choir: rehearsal schedule, various reminders about concerts and socials etc. (remember to use BCC). Keeps minutes in committee, so nobody can complain later.

Treasurer - Deals mainly with money. Takes in membership money and deals with money at concerts. Makes sure people who are owed money get it. Sorts out grant applications and generally deals with YUSU finance.

MD - Deals mainly with music.

AMD - See MD, but slightly less so.

Librarian - Deals mainly with bits of paper. Generally responsible for making sure there is sufficient music around to have a successful rehearsal. This can involve a lot of printing/photocopying, and shifting a lot of paper around. Responsible for making sure that, in time for any performance, there is enough of the appropriate music in enough folders for everyone to sing from. Also is supposed to keep an eye on the larger music library. I would say 'keep it organised and have up-to-date records' but at the moment it would be more a case of 'do something about our really big pile(s) of unsorted music'.

P&P - Deals mainly with publicity. Designing and printing posters and concert programmes. The creating and distributing masses of posters element has taken something of a back seat to word of mouth/Facebook advertising, but you can always do with a few posters. Anything else you can come up with that counts as 'publicity'. Also, usually takes care of Glee Awards if we decide to have them, and if there is no Merch. Officer, takes control of the merchandise order.

Social Secretary - Deals mainly with socials. Get a good idea of what sort of social the choir wants to do, and arrange it.
